Community Guidelines

  • No shows: No shows are strictly prohibited.

  • Report: If your ride sharer doesn't show up, make sure to report to us using the 'report' page in the sidebar.

    • If your ride sharer doesn't show up, make sure to report to us using the 'report' page in the sidebar.

  • Cancel a ride: You can cancel or withdraw from a ride. However, we recommend you to not delete it less than 12 hours before.

  • Ride canceled: If your ride sharer cancels the ride within 12 hours before, you can report them.
